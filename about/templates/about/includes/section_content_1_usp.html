About us

Welcome to ECOSiO - a marketplace with a mission to make eco-friendly cosmetics easily available. Our team consists of young dermatologists, laboratory scientists, ecologists, nutritionists and health-enthusiasts. We believe that both environment and our health always come first - we should never be forced to choose one or another.

We understand sustainability can be an overwhelming and, quite often, an expensive choice. Our internal research team is following all the latest updates about impact of cosmetics full of synthetic ingredients on our health and environment so that you can have a great shopping experience.
